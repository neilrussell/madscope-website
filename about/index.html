About Madscope Marketing

Madscope Marketing is a results-based marketing agency that helps martial arts schools and gyms fill their classes with new students. We are trusted by over 400 schools.

What We Do

We run student enrollment campaigns for martial arts schools and fitness gyms. Our model is simple: we generate the leads, our in-house team handles the follow-up and booking, and you only pay when a prospective student actually walks through your door. We call it Pay Per Show — our incentives are tied to the same outcome you care about: students on the mat, not vanity metrics.

Who We Work With

We work with established school and gym owners who already deliver a great experience and want the right people to find them. Our job is to bridge the gap between a well-run school and a full classroom, with marketing that is measured in enrolled, paying students rather than raw lead counts.

Why Schools Choose Us

  • You only pay when a student shows up — not for clicks, leads, or appointments that never arrive.
  • We run the follow-up — our team books the class so you can focus on teaching.
  • Aligned incentives — we are accountable to the same result you are: students walking in the door.
  • Built for schools and gyms — we understand how enrollment, renewals, and retention actually work.
