Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Who We Are

Madscope Marketing Ltd. is a company registered in Ireland. We operate the website madscopemarketing.com and provide student enrollment marketing services to martial arts schools.

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), Madscope Marketing Ltd. is the data controller. You can contact us at info@madscopemarketing.com.

What Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you book a discovery call or enquire about our services. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and business details.

We also collect usage data automatically through cookies and analytics tools — such as pages visited, time on site, browser type, and referring URLs.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, provide and improve our services, and communicate with you about our offerings. Our lawful basis for processing is legitimate interest (responding to business enquiries and providing contracted services) and, where applicable, your consent (email marketing).

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each of which may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • GoHighLevel — CRM and appointment booking
  • Google Analytics — website analytics
  • Google Ads and Meta Ads — advertising, including conversion tracking pixels placed on this website
  • Email marketing platform — sending marketing and service communications

Some of these providers are based outside the European Economic Area (EEA). Where data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies for analytics and advertising purposes, including pixels from Google and Meta. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may limit some functionality and will prevent advertising measurement from working.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you
  • The right to have inaccurate data corrected
  • The right to have your data erased ("right to be forgotten")
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (Ireland) at dataprotection.ie

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@madscopemarketing.com.

Contact

For any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, email us at info@madscopemarketing.com.
